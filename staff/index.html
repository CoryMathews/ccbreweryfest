SERVING STAFF NEEDED!

Do you love BEER? Are you over 21 years old? Do you have a TABC permit? If you answered yes to these three questions... WE WANT YOU! 

Join us at the Corpus Christi Brewery Festival - 4th Annual on March 7th as a BEERTENDER!

Here are the perks you can look forward to:
- FREE festival glass with beer tickets to enjoy at the festival! (21+)
- Unique behind the scenes experience of the festival, enjoy interacting with all the breweries featured at the event.
- FREE movie vouchers to redeem at our wonderful sponsor Alamo Drafthouse Corpus Christi to thank you for your hard work and support!

This is going to be an event you don't want to miss, so why not be a part of the magic and volunteer with us!

Please email your information, TABC permit, and any questions you have to: info@ccbreweryfest.com

We will send more information regarding the event and there will be a required meeting March 6th to be sure everyone is up to date with all the information and ready to go for the festival on March 7th!
