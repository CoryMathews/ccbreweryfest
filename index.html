Corpus Christi Brewery Festival - 4th Annual

lazybeachbrewing

Breweries and beer lovers from all over the state join us for the 4th annual Corpus Christi Brewery Festival! Hosted at the Art Center of Corpus Christi for the best backdrop any beer festival could ask for!

Corpus Christi Brewery Festival - 4th Annual
Date: Saturday, March 7, 2020
Location: Art Center of Corpus Christi
Address: 100 N Shoreline Blvd, Corpus Christi, TX 78401
VIP Hour: 4:00 PM to 5:00 PM
General Admission: 5:00 PM to 9:00 PM
Tickets

What to Expect
What started as a fun day designed to be able to sample beers from all of the local Corpus Christi breweries has grown into this collaborative event bringing together independent breweries from all over the state. It showcases their artistic creations combined in a fun environment. Along with the breweries crafted creations there will be live entertainment, music, food and a beautiful waterfront view. Bring the whole family and enjoy some of the local creations from our great city and from our amazing state. Beer is an art.

Independent Texas Breweries

While the festival may have started with just our favorite Corpus Christi breweries, we now welcome independent breweries from all over Texas. See all the breweries involved.

Breweries, vendors, bands, live entertainment, or other creators interested in being involved can contact us at: beer@lazybeachbrewing.com
